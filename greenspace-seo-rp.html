Site Audit Findings

6 Critical SEO Problems Found

The audit revealed compounding issues — each one suppressing organic growth. Every card below was a live problem on the site.

01 / Performance
⚡
Poor Core Web Vitals & PageSpeed Performance
Mobile and desktop PageSpeed scores were below Google's recommended standards, with poor Core Web Vitals affecting user experience, crawl efficiency, and organic search visibility. Performance optimization was prioritized.
⚡
Performance Audit
Poor Core Web Vitals
02 / Search Performance
📊
Low CTR & Poor Average Search Position
Google Search Console showed strong impressions but a low click-through rate and weak average positions. Metadata, search intent alignment, and SERP optimization required improvements to increase organic clicks.
📊
Search Performance Audit
Low CTR & Average Position
03 / Authority
🔗
Low Domain Authority & Weak Backlink Profile
Domain Authority remained low with limited high-quality referring domains, restricting keyword growth and overall search visibility. A strategic link-building campaign was required to strengthen domain trust.
🔗
Authority Audit
Domain Authority Report
04 / GEO
🤖
Low AI Visibility & GEO Citation Coverage
The website had limited visibility across AI search engines and large language models. Content lacked entity optimization, authoritative citations, and structured signals required for Generative Engine Optimization (GEO).
🤖
GEO Audit Report
AI Visibility Analysis
05 / Structured Data
🏷️
Missing Schema Markup Implementation
Most pages lacked essential structured data, limiting rich results and search understanding. Key schemas such as Organization, Website, Product, FAQ, and Breadcrumb were missing or incomplete.
🏷️
Structured Data Audit
Missing Schema Markup
06 / Local & Geo
📍
Weak North America Geo Signals
Despite serving all of Canada and the USA, geographic signals were underdeveloped. NAP consistency, Google Business Profile optimization, and hreflang for CA/US were absent or incomplete.
📍
Local SEO Audit
Weak Geo Signals · CA + USA
Execution Strategy

6 Pillars of SEO Execution

Each pillar targeted a specific weakness from the audit — executed in a prioritized, phased sequence.

01 / Content Strategy
📝
3-Month SEO Content & Blog Strategy
Developed a structured three-month content roadmap focused on topical authority, product-focused content, and bottom-funnel conversion. Planned keyword clusters, internal linking, publishing frequency, and content optimization to increase rankings, organic traffic, and qualified leads.
📝
Content Strategy
3-Month Blog Roadmap
02 / Content Strategy
📄
SEO-Optimized Service Page Content Strategy
Developed a search intent-driven content strategy for service pages using keyword research, topical clustering, EEAT principles, structured content hierarchy, schema markup, internal linking, and conversion-focused CTAs to improve rankings and lead generation.
📄
Content Strategy
Service Page SEO
03 / Link Building
🔗
High-Authority Backlink Acquisition Strategy
Executed a white-hat backlink strategy focused on industry directories, guest posting, digital PR, resource pages, partner outreach, and authority citations. Built high-quality referring domains to strengthen domain authority, keyword rankings, and organic search visibility.
🔗
Off-Page SEO
Backlink Strategy
04 / Performance
⚡
Core Web Vitals & PageSpeed Optimization Strategy
Implemented a comprehensive performance optimization strategy by improving Core Web Vitals, optimizing images, reducing JavaScript execution, eliminating render-blocking resources, enabling caching, and optimizing CSS delivery to achieve faster load times and improved PageSpeed scores.
⚡
Performance Strategy
PageSpeed Optimization
05 / Indexing
🗂️
Page Indexing & Crawl Optimization Strategy
Improved Google index coverage by resolving crawl errors, fixing redirects, correcting canonical tags, removing unwanted noindex directives, updating XML sitemaps, and submitting validated URLs to Google Search Console for faster indexing.
🗂️
Indexing Strategy
Page Indexing Fixes
06 / Internal Linking
🔗
Strategic Internal Linking Architecture
Built a hierarchical internal linking framework connecting pillar pages, service pages, industry solutions, blogs, case studies, and resources. Improved crawlability, topical authority, keyword relevance, and user navigation while strengthening page authority distribution.
