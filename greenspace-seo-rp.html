Site Audit Findings

6 Critical SEO Problems Found

The audit revealed compounding issues — each one suppressing organic growth. Every card below was a live problem on the site.

01 / Performance
⚡
Poor Core Web Vitals & PageSpeed Performance
Initial performance analysis revealed poor Core Web Vitals across both Mobile and Desktop. Slow page rendering, render-blocking resources, unoptimized JavaScript, oversized images, and inefficient loading strategies resulted in low PageSpeed scores (Mobile: 18, Desktop: 35), negatively affecting user experience, crawl efficiency, and organic search performance.

Execution Strategy

6 Pillars of SEO Execution

Each pillar targeted a specific weakness from the audit — executed in a prioritized, phased sequence.

01 / Technical
⚡
Next.js Technical SEO & Core Web Vitals
Dynamic pages rendering thin content; 60%+ of pages missing unique metadata; crawl budget wasted on utility routes
Implemented unique metadata per page via Next.js generateMetadata() across all dynamic routes
Fixed canonical tags, hreflang for CA/US geo-targeting, and noindex on utility pages
Rebuilt XML sitemap and resolved all GSC crawl errors across 30+ routes
Optimized next/image with priority loading and deferred non-critical scripts to achieve 90+ PageSpeed
Impact: Full index coverage · 90+ PageSpeed · Zero crawl errors
⚡
Technical SEO Strategy
Next.js & Core Web Vitals
02 / On-Page
📝
Page-Level Keyword & Content Optimization
Service and industry pages using generic copy without keyword targeting; H-tag hierarchy flat and unoptimized
Conducted deep keyword research for 17 industries × 13 services = 220+ target keyword clusters
Rewrote meta titles and descriptions for all pages with intent-matched copy and location modifiers
Optimized H1–H3 hierarchy, added semantic LSI variations and E-E-A-T trust signals
Added FAQs, certifications, and proof points to boost engagement and search relevance
Impact: CTR improved · Target pages moved from Page 3 to Page 1
📝
On-Page Strategy
Keyword & Content Optimization
03 / Content
🗺️
Industry Cluster & Topical Authority Build
No supporting blog content; industry pages were isolated islands with no topical clusters or internal linking structure
Built content cluster maps for all 17 industry verticals — 1 pillar page + 4–6 supporting articles each
Created high-intent blog content targeting warehouse managers' top questions
Implemented strategic internal linking from blog posts to service and product pages
Developed FAQ sections on industry pages to capture People Also Ask placements
Impact: Topical authority increased · Blog now drives qualified traffic to service pages
🗺️
Content Cluster Strategy
17 Industry Verticals
04 / Schema
🏷️
Structured Data & Rich Result Optimization
Zero schema implementation meant no rich results, no FAQ snippets, and reduced SERP real estate vs competitors
Implemented Organization schema with full NAP, social profiles and service area for Canada + USA
Added Service schema across all 13 service pages with descriptions and areaServed markup
Deployed FAQPage schema on industry pages to capture featured snippet opportunities
Added BreadcrumbList and LocalBusiness schema — all validated in Google Rich Results Test
Impact: FAQ rich results in SERPs · Enhanced listings boosting CTR
🏷️
Schema Strategy
5 Schema Types Deployed
05 / Authority
🏆
Backlink & Domain Authority Building
Weak backlink profile with limited referring domains vs established competitors in the industrial automation space
Identified backlink gaps and high-authority link opportunities in the industrial B2B niche
Built quality backlinks through guest posts, digital PR and industry directories
Listed on ThomasNet, Kompass and IndustryNet for B2B authority and citation signals
Created linkable assets — warehouse guides, buying checklists and automation reports
Impact: DA growth · More referring domains · Stronger keyword rankings
🏆
Backlink Strategy
Authority & Domain Growth
06 / Local & Geo
🌎
North America Geo-Targeting Strategy
No geographic differentiation between Canadian and US audiences; Google Business Profile underoptimized; no location-specific landing pages
Fully optimized Google Business Profile with categories, services, Q&A and photo updates
Implemented hreflang for English Canada / English US to differentiate content by market
Created location-specific service content for top Canadian provinces and US industrial hubs
Built local citation consistency across directories for NAP accuracy
Impact: Improved Canadian rankings · US impressions growing quarter-over-quarter
