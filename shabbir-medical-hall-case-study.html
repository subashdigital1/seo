Site Audit Findings

9 Critical SEO Problems Found

The audit revealed compounding issues — each one suppressing organic growth for a pharmacy with thousands of product pages. Every card below was a live problem on the site.

01 / Performance
⚡
Poor Core Web Vitals & Mobile Performance
Mobile performance was below Google's recommended threshold, with a PageSpeed score of 57. Large images, render-blocking resources, and inefficient loading slowed page rendering, negatively impacting Core Web Vitals, user experience, and overall SEO performance.
⚡
Performance Audit
Poor Core Web Vitals
02 / Technical SEO
🔍
Critical Technical SEO Issues Detected
The technical SEO audit uncovered multiple crawl and indexing issues, including duplicate title tags, duplicate meta descriptions, missing title tags, missing H1 tags, unoptimized images, sitemap errors, and orphaned pages. These issues reduced crawl efficiency, affected indexability, and limited overall organic search performance.
🔍
Technical SEO Audit
Crawl & Indexing Issues
03 / Crawl Optimization
🤖
Incomplete Robots.txt Configuration
The robots.txt file was minimally configured and referenced a non-secure HTTP sitemap URL. It lacked proper crawl directives and optimization, which could reduce crawl efficiency and create indexing inconsistencies for search engines.
🤖
Crawl Optimization Audit
Robots.txt Configuration
04 / Google Merchant Center
🛒
Products Not Visible in Google Shopping
Products were not appearing in Google Shopping due to the absence of a properly configured Google Merchant Center feed. This limited product visibility across Google's shopping surfaces and reduced opportunities for organic product discovery.
🛒
Google Merchant Center Audit
Products Not Visible
05 / Search Performance
📉
Low Organic CTR & Average Ranking Position
Google Search Console showed low organic click-through rates (CTR) and an average ranking position outside the top search results. Although impressions were growing, poor keyword rankings and unoptimized metadata limited organic traffic and user engagement.
📉
Search Performance Audit
Low CTR & Rankings
06 / Keyword Rankings
📊
Limited Organic Keyword Visibility
SEMrush Position Tracking revealed low keyword visibility, with only a few keywords ranking in the Top 3 and Top 10 positions. Most target keywords lacked strong search visibility, reducing the site's ability to generate consistent organic traffic.
📊
Keyword Ranking Audit
Low Organic Visibility
07 / Indexing
📑
High Number of Non-Indexed Pages
Google Search Console identified thousands of pages that were not indexed due to redirects, duplicate canonicals, crawl issues, soft 404s, and "Crawled – Currently Not Indexed" status. These indexing problems reduced search visibility and crawl efficiency.
📑
Index Coverage Audit
Non-Indexed Pages
08 / GEO
🤖
No AI Search Visibility & Brand Mentions
The GEO audit showed zero brand mentions across major AI search platforms. The website lacked AI-optimized content, structured entities, and citation signals, resulting in no measurable visibility within AI-generated search responses.
🤖
GEO Audit
AI Search Visibility
9 / Authority
🔗
Low Domain Authority & Backlink Strength
The website had a Domain Authority of 10 with limited ranking keywords and relatively weak backlink authority. Additional high-quality backlinks and stronger topical authority were required to improve trust, rankings, and overall organic performance.
