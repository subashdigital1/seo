Execution Strategy

6 Pillars of SEO Execution

Each pillar targeted a specific weakness from the audit.

⚡

Page Speed & GEO Optimization

Problem: Slow loading pages and weak crawl efficiency affecting GEO visibility
  • →Improved Core Web Vitals by optimizing LCP, CLS and page rendering speed
  • →Compressed images, enabled lazy loading and reduced page weight
  • →Fixed crawl barriers and improved internal linking for better discovery
  • →Optimized site architecture to help search engines understand content
  • →Enhanced crawlability and entity signals to strengthen GEO visibility
📈 Impact: Faster Page Speed · Better Crawlability · Improved GEO Visibility

Internal Linking Strategy

  • →Strengthened internal links to pass authority to key money pages
  • →Connected related content clusters for better topical relevance
  • →Improved crawl paths to help search engines discover content faster
  • →Used optimized anchor text to support SEO, AEO and GEO visibility

Service Page Content Strategy

  • →Added entity-rich content to improve SEO, AEO and GEO visibility
  • →Strengthened trust signals with FAQs, case studies and proof points
  • →Optimized conversion paths to increase enquiries and qualified leads

Backlink Building Strategy
