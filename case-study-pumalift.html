Execution Strategy

4 Pillars of SEO Execution

Each pillar targeted a specific weakness from the audit — executed in a phased, compounding sequence for maximum organic impact.

⚡

Technical SEO & Core Web Vitals Optimization

Problem: Extremely slow mobile performance with poor Core Web Vitals, oversized images, and multiple crawl/indexing issues affecting rankings.
  • → Improved Google PageSpeed mobile score from 24 to 51+ after full technical optimization
  • → Reduced Largest Contentful Paint (LCP) from 8.9s to 3.2s using image optimization & lazy loading
  • → Compressed and converted heavy product images into WebP format to improve loading speed
  • → Minified CSS, JavaScript & enabled browser caching for faster rendering performance
  • → Fixed mobile usability and technical SEO issues impacting indexing and crawl efficiency
📈 Impact: Mobile Score 24 → 51 + · LCP 8.9s → 3.2s · Faster Crawl & Better UX
⚡
Technical SEO Results
PageSpeed 24 → 51+
🛒

Product Page SEO & On-Page Optimization

Problem: 80+ product pages with no SEO optimization, schema, or intent alignment
  • →Rewrote meta titles & descriptions for all 80+ product pages using buyer-intent keywords
  • →Implemented Product, FAQ & BreadcrumbList schema across every product page
  • →Optimized H1–H4 heading hierarchy and keyword placement on each product
  • →Added descriptive, keyword-rich alt tags to 100+ product images for image SEO
📈 Impact: 7 → 31 Top 10 keywords · Rich result eligibility achieved sitewide
🛒
Product Page Results
31 → 74 Top 10 Keywords
📝

Content & Topical Authority Strategy

Problem: Zero content ecosystem — no blog, no clusters, no E-E-A-T signals
  • →Built pillar pages + 14 SEO-optimized blog articles around lifting equipment topics
  • →Optimized every article for search intent, keyword variations & semantic depth
  • →Added author bios, certifications & trust signals to strengthen E-E-A-T
  • →Structured content with concise direct answers for featured snippet eligibility
  • →Created industry buying guides targeting high-intent informational keywords
📈 Impact: Topical authority established · Blog now drives 22% of total organic traffic
📝
Content Strategy Results
Topical Authority Established
🔗

Backlink Strategy & Domain Authority Growth

Problem: Low domain authority, weak backlink profile, and almost zero referring domains impacting keyword rankings.
  • → Built high-quality contextual backlinks through niche directories, guest posting & industry citations
  • → Increased referring domains by acquiring relevant links from manufacturing and logistics websites
  • → Executed anchor-text diversification strategy to maintain natural backlink distribution
  • → Removed toxic and low-quality backlinks affecting overall domain trust signals
  • → Improved internal authority flow to strengthen priority commercial product pages
📈 Impact: Domain Authority 5 → 9 · Quality Backlinks · Stronger Keyword Rankings
